t2p

t2p is an agricultural pricing and calculation app that helps users monitor market prices, calculate break-even and profit targets, and manage alerts for key commodities.

View Privacy Policy

What the app does

  • Displays market price information for supported commodities.
  • Saves calculations for later analysis.
  • Lets users create alerts by commodity, price type, and target value.
  • Sends notifications when alert conditions are met.
