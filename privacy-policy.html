Privacy Policy for t2p

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This privacy policy explains how t2p collects, uses, and stores information when users access the app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address and login details.
  • Calculation data entered by the user.
  • Alert preferences, including commodity, target value, and price type.
  • Notification-related technical information such as device push tokens.

How Information Is Used

  • To authenticate users and provide access to the app.
  • To save and display calculations and alerts.
  • To send price alerts and app notifications.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve app functionality.

Data Storage

Data may be stored using third-party service providers that support app functionality, including authentication, database storage, and notification delivery.

Data Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information may be processed by service providers used to operate the app.

Notifications

If notifications are enabled, the app may store a device push token so that alerts can be sent when relevant market conditions are met.

User Choices

  • Users can stop using the app at any time.
  • Notifications can be disabled in device settings.
  • Users may request account-related data removal by contacting the developer.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, please contact the developer using the support details provided with the app.
